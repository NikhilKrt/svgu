Today, businesses are operating in a highly dynamic marketplace. Therefore, it is important to consider not only business projects but also organisational vision and policies. An MBA program fosters the ability to factor the unique facets of an organization, build and recommend strategies to capitalize on them. Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is an interdisciplinary three years graduate degree program offered by SGVU-DE. On successful completion of the program, students will acquire adequate knowledge and skills in Communication, Logical Reasoning, Business Management, Presentation and Computer Applications besides achieving a remarkable improvement in their overall personality.

Objective
